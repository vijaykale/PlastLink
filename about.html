About Us

Home About us

Established in year 2018, PlastLink Chain Belt is a Manufacturer and Importer of high Quality Plastic Modular Belts,Thermoplastic Slat Chains, Stainless Steel Slat Chains, Conveyor Components, Crate Conveyor Chain, Finger Chain, Sprockets, UHMWPE Wear Strips, UHMWPE Chain Guides, Conveyor Rollers, PU And Pvc Conveyor belts, Bearing Blocks, Wire Mesh Conveyor Belt and more. These products are manufactured from high quality material that is procured from well-known vendors. The offered products can be customized as per the specification provided by our customers. We have been an established and trusted company with an excellent track record for the best customer satisfaction. We have never compromised on the quality and the services provided to the customer. We believe in keeping the customers happy and providing them with products at a very competitive price. Our Director Mr.Sunil having an excellent experience in the belting industry that we are a guide to all our customers.

We are currently operating under the leadership of Mr. Sunil (Director), who has rich knowledge of the Conveyor Components.
